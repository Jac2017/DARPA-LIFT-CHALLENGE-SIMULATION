## Scoring Formula

```
Primary Metric = Payload Weight / Aircraft Weight

- Aircraft weight: 50 lbs
- Payload weight: 120 lbs
- Score: 120 / 50 = 2.4

DARPA's target is a 4:1 ratio (payload 4x heavier than aircraft)

Tiebreakers (in order):
1. Highest payload weight
2. Fastest completion time
```
